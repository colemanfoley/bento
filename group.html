About Notifications

GroupMe is a reply-all application, that means you may get a lot of notifications in rapid succession to your phone if you do not configure it correctly. Here are some guidelines and tips.

  • Do not enable office mode. This turns off notifications for the entire group, and not just for you individually. To turn off notifications, follow the below instructions.
  • Download the GroupMe smartphone app. From here, you can turn off notifications for your account without affecting others.
  • If you are receiving texts, reply with #mute to turn off SMS notifications. When you are ready to receive notifications again, just use #unmute. Again, it is highly recommended to download the smartphone app instead.
